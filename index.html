Desktop & Mobile Games

'Short Games' - Personal Project 2019

HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Brief: To create a collection of games that can be completed quickly.

There are currently 2 games in the collection.

Alien Zap - The player can shoot to eliminate the aliens before they reach the bottom of the screen. The aliens move faster as they are eliminated and as the player progresses through the levels.

Owl Attack - The player must reach 40 points to win the game by tapping the owls as they appear. The owls speed up for every 10 points gained.

Highlights: Alien Zap includes a small particle effect and collision detection, both games include a score board and Owl Attack can be played on both desktop and touch devices.
